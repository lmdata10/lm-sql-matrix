Weekly Activity Patterns

Analyzing user activity by day reveals clear trends. Users are significantly more active midweek and show a distinct drop in activity on weekends. This pattern presents a strategic opportunity for targeted engagement.

Key Insight

Customers are most active on **Tuesday** and least active on **Sunday**. Bellabeat can capitalize on Tuesday's momentum by pushing high-energy challenges and use Sunday for promoting rest, recovery, or light activity to prepare for the week ahead.

The Science of Sleep Efficiency

Sleep quality is as important as quantity. Our data shows a notable dip in sleep efficiency on Sundays, defined as the percentage of time in bed that users are actually asleep. This indicates a potential trend of pre-work-week restlessness.

Key Insight

**Sunday** has the lowest aggregate sleep efficiency. This is a prime opportunity for Bellabeat to deliver value by offering guided meditations, relaxation tips, or wind-down routines on Sunday evenings to help users improve their sleep quality before the week begins.
